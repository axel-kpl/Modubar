Who We Are

Born from the frustration of rigid and bulky sports equipment, we are a collective of fitness enthusiasts and biomechanical engineers.

Our mission is simple: to condense the equivalent of a full commercial gym into a single, elegant, and portable piece of equipment. We believe performance should never be limited by space or gear. With Modubar, we are pushing the boundaries of home and outdoor training.

The Modubar Kit

Everything you need, in one compact format.

  • 1x Stainless Steel Center Bar: The backbone of the system, lab-tested to withstand over 200kg of load.
  • 2x Modular Extensions: Instantly switch from a short curl bar to a standard Olympic barbell.

Material Excellence

Quality is not an option. The Modubar is machined from aerospace-grade carbon steel, selected for its exceptional strength and relative lightness.

Every piece is forged and assembled in Europe, in facilities adhering to strict environmental standards. The knurled grip provides perfect traction without tearing your hands, finished with an exclusive anti-corrosion matte black treatment.
